Program Goal: Program Goal 2: Decrease the social acceptability of tobacco use

Objective:
Obj 2A Increase anti-tobacco attitude among youth and adults
Obj 2B Reduce tobacco sponsorship of sporting, cultural, entertainment and other events in the community, region and state
Obj 2C Reduce tobacco use and promotion in movies, arts, and entertainment
Obj 2D Reduce the proportion of retailers that post point-of-purchase tobacco advertising
