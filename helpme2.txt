<p><strong>Medical Health Systems Change Outcome 1: </strong> Between July 1, 2014, and March 31, 2019, at least 50% of health care organization administrators identified in a catchment area will establish policies and procedures that improve tobacco dependence treatment delivery as recognized by System Strategy 1 (screening systems), System Strategy 2 (training, resources, and provider feedback), System Strategy 3 (dedicated staff), and System Strategy 5 (improve coverage of evidence-based treatments) as described in Chapter 5 of the PHS Guideline, 2008 Update. </p>
